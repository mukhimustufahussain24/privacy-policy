🗑️ Data Deletion Request - Your Grocery App

We take your privacy seriously. If you would like to delete your account and associated data, you can follow the steps below:

How to Delete Your Data
Inside the App:
Go to Settings → Account → Delete My Account. This will permanently remove your data from our servers.

Email Request:
You can also send a request to [support@yourgroceryapp.com] from the email associated with your account. We will process your request within 3–5 business days.
